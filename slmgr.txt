======slmgr switches and options======

View Activation, License, and Expiration Date Information:
slmgr.vbs /dli

To display more detailed license information:
slmgr.vbs /dlv

View the License Expiration Date:
slmgr.vbs /xpr

Uninstall the Product Key:
slmgr.vbs /upk

Uninstall the Product Key from the Registry:
slmgr.vbs /cpky

Set or Change the Product Key:
slmgr.vbs /ipk #####-#####-#####-#####-#####

Activate Windows Online:
slmgr.vbs /ato

Activate Windows Offline:
slmgr.vbs /dti

then provide the Activation ID:
slmgr.vbs /atp ACTIVATIONID

Extend the Activation Timer:
slmgr.vbs /rearm
